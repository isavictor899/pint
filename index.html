Our Core Activities

Clinical Skills Workshops

Hands-on training sessions focusing on suturing, catheterization, phlebotomy, and first aid to bridge the gap between theory and hospital practice.

Community Medical Camps

We organize regular outreach programs to provide free check-ups, basic treatment, and health education to underserved rural communities.

Academic & Research Support

Peer mentorship programs, journal clubs, and revision marathons designed to ensure every student excels in their continuous assessment tests and final exams.
